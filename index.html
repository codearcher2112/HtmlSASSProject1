why Petplan?

Your pets’ love is unlimited. Their coverage should be, too.
All Petplan pet insurance policies cover:

  • accidents

    A broken bone shouldn’t break the bank. That’s why we cover accidents and emergencies — from injured knees and swallowed socks — to stings from bees and cuts from rocks!

  • hereditary + chronic conditions

    Lingering problems pester pets and pocketbooks. Fetch lifelong coverage for chronic issues like allergies, and hereditary conditions like hip dysplasia and cancer.

  • illnesses

    At Petplan, you’ll be covered when your pet is under the weather, including during dental disease, ear infections and stomach bugs.

  • veterinary exam fees

    Petplan saves you more per vet visit by reimbursing you the cost of veterinary exam fees for non-routine care.

No riders. No red tape. No chasing your tail.
That’s our promise to you.
